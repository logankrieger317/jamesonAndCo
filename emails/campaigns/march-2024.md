## Why Seasonal Grooming Matters

When the weather shifts from **cold and wet** to **warm and humid**, your dog's coat and skin face new challenges:

### Winter to Spring Transition Challenges

**Cold & Wet Weather:**
* Thicker coats trap moisture, leading to matting and skin irritation
* Damp fur can harbor bacteria and cause hot spots
* Reduced air circulation under dense winter coats

**Warm & Humid Weather:**
* Increased shedding as dogs lose their winter undercoat
* Higher risk of overheating with a heavy coat
* More prone to skin infections in humid conditions
* Matting becomes more problematic as fur gets wet from spring showers
